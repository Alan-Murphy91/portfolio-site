Starfield

A canvas and a few simple lines of code turns into a journey through a pixelated galaxy. This is my favourite project because of its simplicity, our eyes will only ever see the illusion of travelling through space after having sent a couple of lines to the GPU (a long journey from js!). This is of course not a 'true' 3D experience and the camera is not moving on any axis. The stars are scaled to a 'Z' value which loops to an arbitrary number from zero and resets. The initial X/Y values of the stars are re-calculated based on Z aswell as the size of the star to ensure they scale from small to large.

I used jQuery's window and height methods to turn this into a full-screen, desktop-like experience.

Due to the nature of the project it is NOT RESPONSIVE.
