Crookes Nutrition & Fitness

A client wanted a very specific style and layout for his personal training & nutrition website. I designed wireframes from the simple graphics that were provided and then generated a static site using modern HTML5 semantic tags to improve SEO. To achieve the very exact style I used CSS Grid which is peerless for allowing the developer to mirror wireframes without and complication.

The various sections include payment options for sessions, for this I guided my client through the process of setting up paypal for business and then generated links for each individual service.
